Who are we?

We are the Entrepreneurship Cell at Maharishi Markandeshwar (Deemed to be University), Mullana-Ambala. We are a dedicated team of students and mentors passionate about fostering innovation and entrepreneurship. Our mission is to create a supportive community that empowers students to turn their ideas into successful ventures. We organize events such as workshops, panel discussions, and networking opportunities to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world application. Join us to connect with mentors, industry experts, and fellow entrepreneurs, and to take your entrepreneurial ambitions to the next level.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to foster a dynamic environment where students are inspired to become innovative leaders and successful entrepreneurs. We aim to cultivate an entrepreneurial mindset by providing the tools, resources, and support needed to transform ideas into impactful ventures. We are dedicated to creating a culture of creativity, resilience, and collaboration, bridging the gap between theory and practice. Our goal is to empower students to drive innovation, build sustainable businesses, and contribute positively to society and the economy. Through mentorship and hands-on experience, we envision a thriving ecosystem where every student has the opportunity to realize their entrepreneurial potential.

What We Do?

Through our vision of "Learn, Build and Scale" we implement various initiatives and events in
MMDU to foster the entrepreneurial minds and create a culture of enthralling startups bound for success!

Learn

We believe that the foundation of entrepreneurship lies in continuous learning. Through workshops, seminars, and guest lectures from industry experts, we equip our members with the knowledge and skills necessary to navigate the entrepreneurial landscape. Our educational initiatives cover a wide range of topics, including business strategy, finance, marketing, and innovation.

Build

Turning ideas into reality requires more than just knowledge; it needs practical experience. Our cell provides a platform for students to collaborate on projects, participate in hackathons, and develop their business concepts. We offer mentorship and support in areas such as product development, business planning, and market research, ensuring that every venture has the potential to succeed.

Scale

Scaling a startup is a critical phase that can make or break a business. We support our entrepreneurs in scaling their ventures by connecting them with investors, industry experts, and business networks. Our programs are designed to help startups refine their business models, enhance their market presence, and achieve sustainable growth.

PowerTalks & Panel Discussions

Our PowerTalks and Panel Discussions feature industry leaders, successful entrepreneurs, and domain experts who share their insights and experiences. These events provide students with a unique opportunity to learn from the best in the field, ask questions, and engage in meaningful conversations about entrepreneurship, innovation, and industry trends. Whether it’s a deep dive into a specific topic or a broad discussion on the future of business, these sessions are designed to inspire and educate.

E-Summit

The E-Summit is our flagship event that brings together entrepreneurs, investors, and thought leaders from across the country. This multi-day summit includes keynote speeches, interactive workshops, and networking sessions aimed at exploring emerging trends, sharing success stories, and fostering collaboration. It’s an excellent platform for students to gain knowledge, build connections, and showcase their own entrepreneurial ventures.

Start-Up Fair

The Start-Up Fair is an exciting event where start-ups and aspiring entrepreneurs can exhibit their ideas and innovations. This fair provides a platform for students and young entrepreneurs to showcase their projects, receive feedback from industry experts, and connect with potential investors and partners. It's a vibrant space for exploring new business ideas and discovering the latest trends in the start-up ecosystem.

E-Networking

E-Networking events are designed to facilitate connections between students, entrepreneurs, and professionals from various industries. Through structured networking sessions, mentorship opportunities, and collaborative activities, participants can build valuable relationships, share knowledge, and explore potential collaborations. These events are crucial for expanding one's professional network and gaining insights from a diverse range of perspectives.

Campus Ambassador Program

Our Campus Ambassador Program empowers enthusiastic students to represent the Entrepreneurship Cell across campus and beyond. Ambassadors play a vital role in promoting our events, engaging with their peers, and facilitating communication between the cell and the student body. This program offers valuable leadership experience, networking opportunities, and the chance to make a significant impact within the entrepreneurial community.
